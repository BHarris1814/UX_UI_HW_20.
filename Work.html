My Work

Walk About App

By consolidating travel in one area to book directly with each vendor and to receive reward points, knowing you are getting the best price while choosing your preffered vendor, gives the traveler piece of mind. In addition the application of a walk score accessibility tool to help identify mass transportation, restaurants and venues all in one app helps the traveler make the best choice for their trip.

Redesign of Department of Education Site

The Department of Education current website, prevented the diverse users of educators, parents and students from easily navigating, searching for and accessing important information. The complicated layout, sections and overwhelming amount of links sends the user on a confusing journey to find the information. In addition, the color and text failed the accessibility test limiting usability for individuals with disabilities.

Animal Shelter Redesign Site

The South Utah Valley Animal Shelter has an large amount of animals available for adoption, however, the animals are listed by a number first and no way to filter, sort or search by gender, age or breed. The amount of random listings, provide a very overwhelming glimpse of the animals available for adoption. This makes it hard for the shelter to reduce the amount of available animals ready to be adopted to their forever homes with compatible and compassionate humans.
